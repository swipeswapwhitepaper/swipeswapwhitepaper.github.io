Bulk Bidding

Bidding is very similar to sweeping, you get to choose all the collections you wish to bid on, or specific NFT's from each collection.
Popular tools in the market cannot help you automatically bid on multiple items hands free, you'd have to approve each bid. With SwipeSwap, it is all automatic. A true hands free experience.
All you have to do is make sure you have enough WETH or SOL on any of your wallet(s) and you can begin bidding on the NFT's you want or bid on an entire collection!
