Bulk Sweeping

Sweeping is crucial to NFT collectors. In Ethereum, with competitors like Gem and Genie, our team has actively dove into ways to improve the gas costs in sweeping multiple NFT's at once.
That is to say, being a holder of Tōkun App will mean even more reduced gas costs compared to Gem and Genie, as well as being able to sweep multiple collections at once!
