Partnerships

On phase 1, we've indicated that we're actively building the presence of Tōkun and bridging with impactful projects in the NFT space. Not only are we actively focused on building utility for the NFT space, we're also keen to bring utility to other partnering projects.

Become a Partner

Becoming a partner of Tōkun App also means receiving, benefiting from all the products the Tōkun Team builds. You also become a crucial part of advising the growth and trajectory of Tōkun's development. Thus, this is more than a collaboration. Not only do we bring utility to our own access pass holders, but also to all partnering projects.
