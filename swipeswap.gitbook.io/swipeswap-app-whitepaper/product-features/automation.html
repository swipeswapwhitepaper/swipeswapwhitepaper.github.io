Automation

Wish to mint with a set gwei and utilize your own nodes? Want to mint multiples but have a hard time navigating through different wallets? Welcome to the automation page!
🤖
​
SwipeSwap allows you to generate wallets, distribute funds to each wallet, and automate your minting experience without ever having to mint on your favorite project's site. With just a few clicks, you can process as many mint transactions as you wish.
🍃
​
Currently supporting Ethereum and Solana, more coming soon
